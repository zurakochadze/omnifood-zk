Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

When you start your trial with Omnifood you'll receive full, unlimited access to the plan you chose. You will need to enter your credit card information to begin your trial, but don't worry - we won't charge you anything until the trial ends. You can cancel at any time during the trial period via your Subscriptions settings.

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade or cancel your plan at any time via your Subscription settings.

What is your refund policy?

We guarantee your money back within the first 14 days of your subscription. Contact us at support@omnifood.com to request your refund. Past those 14 days, you can cancel or modify your subscription via your Subscription settings to avoid future charges, but refunds will not be issued.
